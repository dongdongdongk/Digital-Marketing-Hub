### How I Built a Google Maps Scraper in 2 Days Using AI

In the digital marketing world, having access to detailed information about businesses can be incredibly valuable. Recently, a community member shared their experience of building a Google Maps scraper in just two days using AI. This tool helps collect data from Google Maps, like business names, addresses, and phone numbers. Such data is beneficial for marketers who want to understand their competition or find new leads. Although building a scraper sounds complex, using AI and the right tools can make it easier than you think.

### Key Insights into the Project

1. **AI and Tools:** The project used AI to streamline the process. By choosing Python with Playwright, a tool for automating web browsers, the developer could quickly navigate Google Maps and gather data. AI recommended this approach because it efficiently handles complex tasks. This shows that selecting the right tools is crucial in digital marketing, especially when dealing with large datasets.

2. **DataforSEO and Alternatives:** While building the scraper, it was noted that there are existing services like DataforSEO. These services offer ready-made solutions, handling HTML changes and data collection. However, they might not always provide all the details, such as email addresses. Using a custom scraper can offer more control and potentially gather more specific data, which can be vital for tailored marketing strategies.

3. **Sharing and Community:** There was interest in how this tool was made and requests for access or a demonstration. This highlights the importance of sharing knowledge within the digital marketing community. By collaborating and sharing tools or tips, marketers can improve their strategies and stay ahead in the competitive landscape.

### Summary

Building a Google Maps scraper can be quick and effective with the right AI tools. Python with Playwright simplifies data collection, while options like DataforSEO offer ready-made solutions. Sharing such innovations within the community helps everyone improve their marketing strategies.
